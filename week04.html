Week 04 – Errors and Usability Issues

This week focused on understanding user errors and how interface design can help prevent or recover from them.

Dix et al. classify errors into slips and mistakes, where slips occur during execution and mistakes occur due to incorrect goals or understanding (Dix et al., Chapter 8).

Types of Errors

  • Slips – correct intention, wrong action
  • Mistakes – incorrect intention or plan

Effective systems provide feedback, clear error messages, and easy recovery mechanisms to improve overall usability.
